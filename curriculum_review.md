# School Curriculum Review
**Compiled:** June 2026  
**Purpose:** Tutor app subject planning for Aruna (Year 10, Wilmington Academy) and Arjun (Year 8, Wilmington Grammar School for Boys)

## Aruna — Year 10, Wilmington Academy (Leigh Academy Wilmington), Dartford

Wilmington Academy is a non-selective academy in Dartford operating the IB Middle Years Programme (MYP) in KS3, transitioning to GCSEs/BTECs in Key Stage 4 (Years 10–11).

### Compulsory Core Subjects (All Year 10 students)
- English Language
- English Literature
- Mathematics
- Combined Science (or separate sciences for some students)

### Compulsory Pathway Subjects (all students choose one from each group)
- **Humanity:** History **or** Geography
- **Language:** French **or** Spanish *(or Finance/LIBF for alternative pathway students)*

### Optional / Vocational Subjects (students choose from available options)
The KS4 curriculum at Wilmington Academy offers three routes — **creative**, **academic**, or **vocational**:

| Creative | Academic | Vocational |
|---|---|---|
| Art | History | Health and Social Care |
| Music | Geography | Travel and Tourism |
| Performing Arts | Citizenship | Business / Financial Studies |
| Digital Photography | Further Maths | IT / Creative Media |
| | | Catering / Food |
| | | Engineering |
| | | Product Design |
| | | Sport (BTEC) |
| | | Horticulture (Year 11 alternative pathway) |

### Notes
- The school uses GCSEs and BTECs/Level 2 vocational qualifications across Years 10–11.
- Some students follow an **alternative curriculum pathway**: Finance (LIBF) replaces a Language GCSE in Year 10; Horticulture may replace a Humanity in Year 11.
- The school follows an academy-specific timetable; exact option blocks depend on the student's individual choices made in Year 9.

**Source:** [wilmingtonacademy.org.uk/curriculum-and-assessment/our-subjects/](https://wilmingtonacademy.org.uk/curriculum-and-assessment/our-subjects/)

## Arjun — Year 8, Wilmington Grammar School for Boys (WGSB), Dartford

WGSB is a selective grammar school. Year 8 sits within Key Stage 3.

### Year 8 Subjects (all compulsory)

| Subject | Periods per Fortnight |
|---|---|
| English | 8 |
| Mathematics | 8 |
| Science | 9 |
| French | 8 (MFL) |
| Spanish | Introduced in Year 8 |
| History | 3 |
| Geography | 3 |
| Religious Studies | 2 |
| Fine Art | 2 |
| Music | 2 |
| Design & Technology | 4 |
| Computer Science | 2 |
| Business Studies | part of curriculum |
| PE & Games | 4 |
| Life Programme (PSHE/RSE/Careers) | 1 |

**Total: 60 periods per fortnight**

### Notes
- Spanish is **added** in Year 8 (students study both French and Spanish in Year 8–9).
- Business Studies is included in KS3 at WGSB.
- Drama appears in the timetable from Year 8 (2 periods per fortnight in some year groups).
- The **Life Programme** covers PSHE, RSE, Careers and pastoral education — delivered in form time and drop-down sessions.
- In Years 10–11, students continue core GCSEs plus four option choices from: Business, Computer Science, Construction & the Built Environment, Design & Technology, Drama, Fine Art, Food Preparation & Nutrition, French, Geography, History, Physical Education, Spanish.

**Source:** [wgsb.co.uk/learning/](https://wgsb.co.uk/learning/)
